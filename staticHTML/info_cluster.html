Chunking Strategy Training

Expand your working memory through strategic chunking.

You'll see sequences of items (numbers, letters, colors, or patterns).

Your goal is to memorize and recall them accurately.

The key skill: Group items into meaningful chunks!


Chunking Example:

Instead of: 1 9 4 5 2 0 2 4 (8 separate items)

Think: 1945 | 2024 (2 chunks of years)


Instead of: R E D B L U E G R N

Think: RED | BLUE | GRN (3 color chunks)


Training Phases:

  • Memorization - View the sequence carefully
  • Recall - Reconstruct the sequence from memory
  • Feedback - See your accuracy and chunking efficiency

Tip: Look for patterns, repetitions, and natural groupings!
